## WikiMatrix

Selected English and Chinese example sentence pairs are derived from
WikiMatrix v1, a parallel corpus mined from Wikipedia and distributed through
Facebook Research LASER and OPUS. Only manually reviewed pairs from the
original scored English–Chinese TSV are included.

WikiMatrix is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0
International. The corpus should be cited as:
